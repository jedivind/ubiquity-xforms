9.1.1.a2 group element inside case element of a switch When you activate the Show Out Case trigger you must see "You are now in the Out case" output to the screen and a Show In Case trigger. When you activate the Show In Case trigger you must see "You are now in the In case" output to the screen and a Show Out Case trigger. When either switch is activated you must see an xforms-disabled message and an xforms-enabled message.
