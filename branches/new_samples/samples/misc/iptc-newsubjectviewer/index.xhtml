IPTC Codes

The International Press and Telecommunications Council (IPTC) have a number of standards that relate to news delivery. One of their sets of information provides for categorisation. This sample shows how you might represent a very large list (the list of subjects that the article may be about) when you actually need to allow for multiple selection within that list.
