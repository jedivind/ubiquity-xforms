Acura 2005 blue 11.1.p standalone attribute of submission element When you activate the Submit With Standalone true submit control you must see a standalone declaration in the form data. It may appear as the value " standalone="yes" " or " standalone="true" ". When you activate the Submit With Standalone false submit control you must see a standalone declaration in the form data. It may appear as the value " standalone="no" " or " standalone="false" ". When you activate the Submit Without Standalone submit control you must not see a standalone declaration in the form data.
