Acura 2005 blue 11.1.p standalone attribute of submission element When you activate the "Submit standalone=true" submit control you must see a standalone="true" attribute in the submitted data. When you activate the "Submit standalone=yes" submit control you must see a standalone="yes" attribute in the submitted data. When you activate the "Submit without standalone" submit control you must see no standalone attribute in the submitted data. Submit standalone=true Submit standalone=yes Submit without standalone
